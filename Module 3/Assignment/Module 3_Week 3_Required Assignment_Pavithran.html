About Course: Full Stack Development with AI Programme (FSAI)

In this programme, you will explore the frontiers of web development, mastering both front-end and back-end technologies while gaining a deep understanding of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its integration into full stack development. It will empower you to streamline tasks such as code generation, testing, and debugging while allowing you to focus on complex logic and creative problem-solving. Through an immersive learning experience that includes pre-recorded video lectures from leading faculty, real-world case studies, and a capstone project, you will develop the expertise needed to build robust, adaptable applications that meet rising user expectations. From managing client-side and server-side programming to crafting visually striking web applications, you will emerge as a highly proficient full-stack developer, ready to thrive in the fast-paced world of technology.
